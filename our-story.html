Our Development Journey

Phase 1: The Initial Prototype

January 2023

Our journey began with a basic proof-of-concept prototype. Using off-the-shelf components and simple machine learning models, we demonstrated that AI could indeed identify different types of waste materials. This first prototype achieved 65% accuracy - not perfect, but enough to prove the concept was viable.

Key Achievements:

  • Proved AI could identify waste materials
  • Built basic mechanical sorting mechanism
  • Established core team and vision

Phase 2: Enhanced Design & Engineering

March 2023

Building on our initial success, we focused on improving the mechanical design and AI accuracy. We integrated better sensors, refined our machine learning algorithms, and redesigned the sorting mechanism for more reliable operation. This phase saw our accuracy jump to 78%.

Key Achievements:

  • Improved accuracy to 78%
  • Enhanced mechanical reliability
  • Better sensor integration
  • Won first university innovation award

Phase 3: Advanced Features & Competitions

June 2023

This phase had a design close to our final product, but instead of a rotating base, it featured a surface that tilted in different directions to slide disposed items into different bins. Our system now achieved 89% accuracy and could process waste at speeds comparable to human operators.

Key Achievements:

  • Achieved 89% accuracy
  • Won 3rd place at National Innovation Challenge
  • Added mobile app and cloud analytics
  • Processed waste at human-equivalent speeds

Phase 4: Production-Ready Design

September 2023

As we prepared for market entry, we focused on making our system production-ready. This involved extensive testing, safety certifications, and optimization for mass manufacturing. We achieved 94% accuracy and designed for easy maintenance and scalability.

Key Achievements:

  • Reached 94% accuracy
  • Obtained safety certifications
  • Designed for mass production
  • Won 1st place at Regional Startup Competition

Phase 5: Testing and Refinement

December 2023

After achieving a production-ready design, we rigorously tested our system in real-world environments. This phase focused on identifying edge cases, refining our AI algorithms, and optimizing the system for various waste streams. We achieved 96% accuracy and improved the system's robustness and reliability.

Key Achievements:

  • Achieved 96% accuracy in real-world tests
  • Improved system robustness and reliability
  • Optimized for various waste streams

Current State: Market-Ready Solution

December 2023 - Present

Today, OscarAI stands as a fully developed, market-ready solution for intelligent waste sorting. Our system achieves 96% accuracy, processes waste faster than human operators, and is ready for deployment in various environments from offices to industrial facilities.

Current Capabilities:

  • 96% sorting accuracy
  • Faster than human processing
  • 24/7 operation capability
  • Cloud-based monitoring and analytics
  • Scalable for various facility sizes
